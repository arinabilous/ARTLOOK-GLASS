    Glass Wall Cladding

    Glass Wall Cladding is a versatile product that provides coloured glass surfaces for residential and commercial interiors.

    Glass is perfect material for lift and entrance areas in commercial interiors.

    Glass wall cladding is ideal for a variety of residential and commercial applications including:

    • Commercial Reception Areas
    • Restaurants
    • Shower Rooms
    • Kitchens

    The cladding is produced using a low iron glass that comes in any shape or size.
    The company's service also enables you to incorporate texture, images and colour patterns into the glass.
    The cladding can be cut around any shaped wall, sockets, taps, wiring and sloped ceilings..

    The glass is adhered to a prepared wall using an appropriate adhesive.
    The glass can be drilled and fixed to the wall using the correct equipment so that it can be removed or replaced at a later date.

    Apart from modernising your home or workplace, the glass is also extremely hygienic, impervious to wear, easy to maintain and a perfect substitute for tiles and grout in any room.

    All Global Glassworks splashbacks are made from the highest quality Pilkingtons toughened Low Iron 4mm & 6mm glass with a polished edge.

    Individual glass splashback designs can be installed anywhere in your property. Global Glassworks specialist fitters are experienced to carry out any client requirements.
